AI Quickstart Guide

  1. 🔹 What is AI — Artificial Intelligence (AI) helps automate tasks, create text, images, and videos. Simply put: AI is a tool that can do work for you if you instruct it correctly.
  2. 💬 How to talk to AI — AI understands clear instructions. The more precise your task description, the better the results. Example:
    ❌ "Write something about marketing"
    ✅ "Write a short Instagram post about a marketing strategy for a small coffee shop."
  3. ✍️ How to ask good prompts — Use context and format. Always clarify: what, for whom, in which style.
    Example: "Create 3 name ideas for a travel YouTube channel in a fun style."
  4. ⚠️ Common beginner mistakes — Typical mistakes:
    • Too vague prompts
    • Expecting perfect results
    • Ignoring AI instructions
    • Not fact-checking
    Tip: Be specific and always review AI output.
  5. 🛠️ Best AI tools to start — Recommended tools for beginners:
    • ChatGPT — text, ideas, guidance
    • MidJourney — image generation
    • Runway — video and animation
    • Perplexity — quick answers and search
    • Notion AI — organization and notes
